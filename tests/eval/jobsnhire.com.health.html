Better Health For America: What You Need To Know About CVS Health

TAG:CVS Health, CVS Pharmacy, Healthcare, CVS Health Platinum Whitening Kit
By Jose de la Cruz

CVS Health is all about helping people who are treading a path to better health. Basically, this business entity is a pharmacy innovation company with a clear goal of providing the best in health care to its customers.

Currently, there are millions of people around the world being impacted by the products, services and health initiatives that CVS Health disburses. The company claims that this is its sole purpose and the thing that inspires it to do what they are doing.

There are four main areas where CVS Health accomplishes this objective:

1.    CVS/pharmacy

For over 50 years, this company has provided customers with products and services that assisted them in gaining better health. Aside from pharmaceuticals, CVS Health also has trend beauty departments, photo labs and general merchandise

2.    CVS/caremark

The company also provides pharmacy benefit management and prescription mail order services. It uses a combination of clinical interventions, discounted drug purchase deals and formulary management techniques to help control drug costs for CVS members and customers.

3.    CVS/minuteclinic

This company also provides health care access in its more than 1,100 locations. There are company nurses who diagnose illnesses, injuries and skin disorders, and then provide the appropriate wellness services, including physicals, vaccinations and monitoring of chronic conditions.

4.    CVS/specialty

CVS Health also helps those diagnosed with complex and rare health conditions. Apart from providing the tools and services that patients need, CVS also fills up specialty prescriptions so that patients can effectively manage their medication therapy, and thus be in the best of health.

It seems that the service provided by CVS Health to the public is being rewarded bountifully. A recent company financial report revealed that it had a strong fourth quarter and full year profit growth for 2015.

The company's net revenues increased 11 percent to a record of $41.1 billion. Its operating profit increased 17.6 percent to $2.7 billion. It seems CVS Health is really enjoying the confidence of its millions of customers around the world.
