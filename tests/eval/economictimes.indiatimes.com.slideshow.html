    The iPhone SE is in India, here's all we know

    ET Online|
    09 Jun 2020, 02:32 PM IST
    1/5

    ​Not a flagship

    The latest iPhone from Apple is not what you would expect - a successor to the 2016 SE - it is designed to look like the iPhone 8 from 2017, but is a massive upgrade on it.

    Image credit: www.apple.com/in/

    Agencies
    2/5

    ​Pocket friendly

    First off, its form factor is a surprise - a 4.7 in (diagonal) display - puts it in the smaller screen size category, with most of the smartphones in the market are closer to the 6in mark, and some are over it.

    Image credit: www.apple.com/in/

    Agencies
    3/5

    Powerful package

    But, the iPhone SE fits in so much more in the tiny form factor - cramming in Apple’s latest A13 bionic chip and bringing back the much-missed home button and with it, TouchID.


    Image credit: www.apple.com/in/

    Agencies
    4/5

    ​Throwback to touch ID

    In a departure from their flagships, the iPhone SE gets a fingerprint sensor, instead of FaceID - Apple’s proprietary face unlock system.
    Image credit: www.apple.com/in/

    Agencies
    5/5

    ​Cheaper than the rest

    All of this - coupled with better cameras - comes in at Rs 42,900, less than half the price of Apple’s flagships.
